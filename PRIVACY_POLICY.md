# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 17, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how **[APP_NAME]** (the "App") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Email address when you create an account (optional)
- **User Content**: Flashcards, collections, and study data you create within the App
- **Contact Information**: Name and email when you submit bug reports or feature requests
- **Profile Information**: First name and notification preferences

### 2. Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the App (analytics via PostHog)
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, app version
- **Error Reports**: Crash logs and diagnostic data (via Sentry)
- **Purchase Information**: Subscription status and transaction history (via RevenueCat)

### 3. Photos and Camera Access

- **Images**: We may access your photo library or camera only when you choose to add images to your flashcards. Images are stored locally on your device and optionally synced to your account if you enable synchronization.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Provide and improve the App's functionality
- Enable synchronization of your data across devices (optional)
- Process in-app purchases and manage subscriptions
- Send you daily review reminders (if you enable notifications)
- Respond to your support requests and feedback
- Analyze app usage to improve user experience
- Monitor and fix technical issues

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

- Your flashcards and study data are primarily stored **locally on your device** using SQLite
- You maintain full control over your data

### Cloud Synchronization (Optional)

- If you create an account, your data can be synchronized to our servers (powered by Supabase)
- All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard HTTPS
- Your password is hashed and never stored in plain text

### Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

#### Supabase (Optional Cloud Sync)

- Purpose: Account authentication and data synchronization
- Data Stored: Email, user ID, flashcard content
- Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

#### RevenueCat (In-App Purchases)

- Purpose: Subscription management
- Data Shared: Anonymous user ID, purchase history
- Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

#### PostHog (Analytics)

- Purpose: Understand app usage patterns
- Data Collected: Anonymous usage statistics, feature adoption
- Privacy Policy: https://posthog.com/privacy
- Note: We do NOT enable session recording

#### Sentry (Error Monitoring)

- Purpose: Detect and fix technical issues
- Data Collected: Crash reports, error logs, device metadata
- Privacy Policy: https://sentry.io/privacy/

#### Web3Forms (Contact Forms)

- Purpose: Bug reports and feature requests
- Data Shared: Name, email, message content
- Privacy Policy: https://web3forms.com/privacy

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Deletion

- You can view all your data within the App
- You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings
- Local data is deleted immediately
- Cloud data (if sync enabled) is permanently deleted from our servers

### Data Portability

- You can export your flashcards at any time
- Your data is yours, and you can take it with you

### Notifications

- You can enable or disable daily review reminders in Settings
- You can change notification time preferences

### Analytics Opt-Out

- Analytics are only enabled in production builds
- Your data is anonymized and aggregated

## Children's Privacy

[APP_NAME] is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Retained until you delete it or uninstall the App
- **Cloud Data**: Retained until you delete your account
- **Analytics Data**: Retained for 90 days in aggregated form
- **Error Logs**: Retained for 90 days

## International Data Transfers

If you use the synchronization feature, your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located in different countries. By using the App and enabling sync, you consent to such transfers.
